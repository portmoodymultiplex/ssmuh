About The Founder

Dawar Zada, PREC*

Dawar is Team Lead at Zada Group, operating under Stonehaus Realty Corp. in Coquitlam, BC. He's not just a real estate specialist — he's a multiplex pioneer who walked the walk before building SSMUH.ca.

As the first homeowner in Port Moody to move a multiplex application forward, Dawar has intimate knowledge of municipal rules, timelines, and practical realities. He sat with City of Port Moody planning staff to understand the SSMUH rules, completed specialized professional training in SSMUH development, and now guides clients through the entire process.

His Career Philosophy

"I've never been a 'this sale' guy. I want to know where you want to end up — then build the road to get you there."

That philosophy has shaped his entire career. He's built a reputation for helping clients move up the wealth ladder step by step: $400K condo → townhouse → detached → multiplex. It's not about moving quickly; it's about moving smartly.

Life Outside Real Estate

Dawar lives in the Tri-Cities with his wife, three kids, and Bear the Samoyed. His family values — keeping loved ones close, building generational wealth, and caring about neighbourhood — directly inform how he guides families through SSMUH decisions.

The Process

The Wealth Roadmap

How families typically progress from their first home to a multiplex — and how to do it without selling the family property.

1

First Home

Start with a modest condo or townhouse. Build equity, learn the market.

2

Townhouse/Duplex

Use equity from #1 as down payment. Now you own two units or have rental income.

3

Detached Home

Scale up with more land. Begin thinking about density potential on future property.

4

SSMUH-Ready Lot

Purchase a lot with SSMUH potential. Check municipal rules and your timeline.

5

Design & Develop

Work with architects, city, and contractors. Create your custom multiplex.

6

Generational Asset

Build long-term wealth: live in one, rent others, or structure for your family's future.
